About the Competition

The Rice Business Energy Case Competition (REC) 2025 invites MBA students nationwide to engage in a case competition focused on solving pressing energy challenges through innovative business opportunities.

As organizations face increasing investment choices around energy, prospective business leaders will evaluate their risks and opportunities, balancing sustainability and reliability needs in creative ways that provide sustained competitive advantage while adapting to the rapidly evolving energy landscape.

Our competition provides a platform for talented students to demonstrate their aptitude in developing practical, real-world approaches to energy-related business challenges.

Competition Details

Event timeline

  • Team registration and case prompt release: September 2025
  • Online submission due: October 2025
  • Finalists announced: November 2025
  • Final competition and award ceremony: November 2025

Format

  • REC works with industry leaders to finalize a case theme
  • MBA case solutions judged by professional panel
  • Finalists compete at Rice University, meet industry leaders
  • Awards presentation and networking follows at the 2025 Rice Energy Finance Summit

Past participants

  • 100+ teams from leading business schools
  • 29 universities including top-tier MBA programs
  • 18 states represented, creating a truly national competition

Theme

Currently being finalized and likely to focus on the impacts of AI and Machine Learning. Previous themes included investment proposals to enhance electric infrastructure, grid flexibility, and manage demand growth.
