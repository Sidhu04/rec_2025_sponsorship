About the Competition

The Rice Business Energy Case Competition (REC) 2025 invites MBA students nationwide to engage in a case competition focused on solving pressing energy challenges through innovative business opportunities.

As organizations face increasing investment choices around energy, prospective business leaders will evaluate their risks and opportunities, balancing sustainability and reliability needs in creative ways that provide sustained competitive advantage while adapting to the rapidly evolving energy landscape.

Our competition provides a platform for talented students to demonstrate their aptitude in developing practical, real-world approaches to energy-related business challenges.

Competition Details

Event Timeline

  • Team Registration & Case Prompt Release: September
  • Online Submission Due: October
  • Finalists Announced: November 13
  • Final Competition & Award Ceremony: November 14

Format

  • Teams submit solutions online
  • Judging and finalist selection
  • Finalists compete in person at Rice University
  • Networking, presentations, and awards

Participants

  • 57 registrations, 34 team submissions
  • 13 universities across 12 states
  • 5 finalist teams selected for in-person competition
  • 52 expert judges from industry and academia

Theme

Present investment proposals that enhance electric infrastructure, grid flexibility, and demand growth, addressing capacity constraints, innovative solutions, and sustainability.
